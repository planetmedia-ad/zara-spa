About Zara Spa

At Zara Spa, we are dedicated to providing an exceptional experience tailored to your beauty and wellness needs. Formerly known as Iyat Salon and Spa, we have rebranded and relocated to a larger, more luxurious space to better serve you.

Founded by a certified medical esthetician with over 15 years of experience, Zara Spa specializes in advanced medical facials and skin treatments. Our founder is proud to have been nominated for Best Brows and Lashes in Ottawa, reflecting our commitment to excellence in every service we offer.

In addition to our facial and skin care expertise, we also provide professional face, ear, and body piercing services, ensuring a comprehensive approach to beauty and self-expression.

Join us at Zara Spa for a rejuvenating experience that goes beyond the ordinary. We look forward to welcoming you into our new space and helping you achieve your beauty goals.
